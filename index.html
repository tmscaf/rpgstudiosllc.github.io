About RPG Studios LLC

RPG Studios LLC is a software and mobile app development company established in 2018 and headquartered in Ohio, United States. We specialize in creating tools that empower users — from drone pilots to tech-savvy consumers — with innovative and efficient software solutions.

Waypoint

A web-based mission planner for DJI drones. Allows users to configure waypoints, actions, and export for DJI Fly compatibility. Developed to support CSV/XML formats and multi-step actions per point.

PageChange (iOS)

Stay ahead with instant alerts for website content changes. PageChange monitors specified URLs for keyword matches, notifying users the moment changes are detected. Ideal for tracking product restocks or news updates.

Drone Camera Analyzer (iOS)

Optimize your drone footage using real-time exposure readings, ND filter recommendations, and advanced light analysis. Includes environment data integration and intelligent camera profile mapping per drone model.
